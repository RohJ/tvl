What is this about?

These courses are designed to help you begin the journey of living a victorious life by helping you lay the right foundations as you grow stronger in your walk with Christ. As part of the course, you will be added into a group of 5 - 10 people anchored by a host to ensure that it remains interactive and promotes discussion within the session.
