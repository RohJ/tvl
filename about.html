About

Know the author

Based in Bangalore, India, Rev. Victor D'Monte has committed his life to fulfil the calling that God has placed upon him. Having founded Adonai Ministries in 1986, he holds the position of Senior Pastor in Adonai Church, taking the Gospel of Jesus to people across India and overseas. He firmly believes that every gift God has given to the Body of Christ must be used for the common purpose of discipling people and building His Church.

God has used him and his wife, Annie, extensively in the Healing and Deliverance ministry, ministering to those who are emotionally, physically and spiritually broken. Over the years they have felt the call to minister to pastors, leaders and congregations helping them find their purpose and fulfil their God-given destiny.

Inspiring him to author the books, 'Satan and his kingdom Exposed!' and 'Discerning of Spirits', as well as two workbooks titled 'Keys to Victorious Living' and 'Building a Godly Foundation' that are used as useful tools to disciple believers.

Adonai Church

At Adonai, we believe the entire Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God. We believe in the one true God, eternally existing in three distinct persons (Father, Son and Holy Spirit) each of whom is fully and equally God. The Church is the Body of Christ, of which Jesus is the Head and its members being those who have truly received Christ by faith. We as a church exist for the sole purpose of discipling nations. Our heart's desire and vision is to Touch Lives, Reach Communities and be an Impact to Cities and beyond.

Healing and Deliverance Conference

The Healing and Deliverance Conference is a 7-day residential programme that aims at touching and transforming lives, ministering to those who are emotionally, physically and spiritually broken. This ministry of inner healing and deliverance is not an end in itself but a means for effective discipleship. We desire to see people restored and able to disciple nations through these conferences.
